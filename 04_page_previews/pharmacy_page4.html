Strategic Recommendations

Optimize Promotion Strategy

Reduce blanket promotions. Target promotions to low-margin categories to improve overall mix. Consider volume thresholds before applying discounts.

Expand Generic Portfolio

Generics deliver 5pp higher margins (32.4% vs 27.4%). Increase generic SKU count by 20% focusing on high-volume therapeutic areas.

Premium Product Focus

Wellness and Personal Care show 33%+ margins. Cross-sell these categories to prescription customers to boost basket value and profitability.

Promotional Efficiency Metrics

Implement ROI tracking for all promotions. Set minimum margin floor of 22% for promotional pricing to protect overall profitability.
